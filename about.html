A Little About Us:
The Copper Moon was found in May 2011. The owner, James Orlando, who has always been infatuated by the beautiful craft of being a barista, came to downtown Cleveland to share with you his fresh, tasty and elegantly brewed drinks.

Came from the baking legacy of the Orlando, James carries on the high standard when it comes to his crafts. You can expect every item you get at the Copper Moon is made from scratch, with the utmost attention to detail and the pride of a barista veteran.
